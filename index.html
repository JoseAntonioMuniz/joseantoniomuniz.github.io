Postdoctoral Researcher · Financial Economics

José Antonio Muñiz, Ph.D.

I am a postdoctoral researcher currently in The School of Public Policy at LSE, London, UK. My work spans policy topics such as sustainability, monetary policy, as well as quantitative finance, with a focus on how quantifiable policy produces change. I hold a Ph.D. in Financial Economics from the University of Bath (Supervised by Dr. Charles Larkin and Prof. Shaen Corbet).
